Tips for activities


Here we have put together a few tips for activities, for all of you who plan to arrive earlier and/or stay longer.


Rovereto sightseeing


  • The historic old town

    • Information
    • You should definitely take a walk through the historic center of Rovereto.


  • MART, museum of modern art

    • The MART houses the most important collection of futuristic art in Italy
    • and is just a short walk from the old town center


  • Museo Storico Italiano della Guerra, the historical war museum

    • is one of the most important war museums in Italy
    • is located in the castle of Rovereto right above the old town


  • Campana dei Caduti, the bell for the fallen

    • Memorial site on a hill above Rovereto
    • From here you have a view over the smoked valley of Rovereto


Hiking in and around Rovereto


Rovereto - and the province of Trentino in general - are a hiker’s paradise. If you live in Rovereto, you can start a wonderful hike practically right from your front door. There are various websites with hiking routes in and around Rovereto as well as for the whole province of Trentino. You can find a very nice overview here.


One of our favourite routes for a short weekend hike leads along an old mountain path from Rovereto via Noriglio to the “Laghetti del Leno”, a place in the mountains were the river “Leno” forms small lakes. You can also take a dip in the water there. Mehr Infos hier.

You can find a hiking map here: Rovereto - Laghetti del Leno.


Excursion to Lake Garda


Rovereto is only about 20 km from the northern shore of Lake Garda. From Rovereto, there are good bus connections (e.g., starting at the train station) that will take you to Torbole or Riva, the first two towns on the northern shore of the lake. The roads to Lake Garda are almost always quite busy in the holiday season, which is why it’s worth to start your trip early.


There are several ferry connections from Torbole. From there, you can take wonderful boat trips that take you to the most beautiful places on Lake Garda. Particularly beautiful are, for example, the tours to Malcesine oder Limone.
